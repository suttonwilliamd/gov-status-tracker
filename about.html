About This Project

Honest disclosure: I'm currently experiencing homelessness while working on this project. I used DeepSeek-R1 to help build this service status monitor in under an hour. While the AI assisted with the technical implementation, the vision and final execution are my own.

Why I Built This

As someone without stable housing, reliable access to government services is crucial. This project helps everyone quickly verify the status of essential .gov websites. Transparency in public services benefits us all.
